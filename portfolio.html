Portfolio

What to Include

  • 2–4 strong projects that match your target roles.
  • Clear role and impact, not just deliverables.
  • Before/after snapshots, metrics, or user outcomes where possible.

Interview Prep

  • Prepare a 5–8 minute walkthrough.
  • Anticipate questions about decisions, trade-offs, and impact.
  • Link to live demos or code repos where applicable.
